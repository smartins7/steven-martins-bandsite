Xavier de Rosnay & Gaspard Auge

Xavier De Rosnay and Gaspard Auge had a few mutual friends, who happened to run a record label, seeking tracks for a compilation. With an immediate connection, the pair decided to try their hand at making a song. That's when they created their breakout single, We Are Your Friends - a feel good, funk-filled, instant anthem that lost the remix contest, but won the attention of Pedro Winter, director of newly founded Ed Banger Records, who boasted Daft Punk as former clients. One week later, they were in his office signing a record deal.
